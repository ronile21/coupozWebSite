How it works

Four steps, from setup to sale

CoupoZ is designed to be fast and intuitive for everyone in the ecosystem.

01

Store creates a campaign

Retailers define the offer, select a time window, and publish - directly from the CoupoZ dashboard. No technical skills needed.

02

Location activates the offer

When a shopper enters the mall or passes by a store, CoupoZ detects proximity and unlocks relevant coupons in real time.

03

Shopper redeems the coupon

The consumer taps to claim the deal and shows it at the register for instant redemption - no printing, no hassle.

04

Everyone sees the results

Stores see their campaign stats. Malls see anonymized, aggregated traffic and redemption trends. Clear, actionable, privacy-safe.
