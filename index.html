The Problem

Nearby customer intent goes unmet — until now

Physical venues have always struggled to prove the real business value of promotions. The data never connects.

Hard to measure results

Traditional print coupons and flyers offer limited visibility. Venues often struggle to connect promotional activity with business outcomes.

Disconnected promotion operations

Print, signage, and digital channels often sit in separate workflows, making promotion operations harder to manage across venues.

Shoppers miss offers

Shoppers walk past stores every day without knowing about relevant nearby offers. Disconnected channels make timely offer visibility difficult.
